Descripción detallada

This class removes surplus individuals based on bounding hybercube, which can be calculated in objective or decision space. But i guess currently you can't toggel that. But here the hybercubes are static, e.g. theey are not recalculated after an individual is removed. Created by IntelliJ IDEA.
