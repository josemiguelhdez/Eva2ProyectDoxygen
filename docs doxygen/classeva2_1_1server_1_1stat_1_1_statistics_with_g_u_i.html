Descripción detallada

A statistics class to plot fitness curves in client-server mode. Mainly, arrays of GraphWindows and Graphs are managed and the selected data fields are plotted. TODO: this could finally be cleanly reduced to an InterfaceStatisticsListener - without inheriting from AbstractStatistics.
