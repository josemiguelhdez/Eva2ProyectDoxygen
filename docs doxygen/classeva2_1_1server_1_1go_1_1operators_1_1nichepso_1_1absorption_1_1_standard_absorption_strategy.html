Descripción detallada

Particles are absorbed into a subswarm when they move into an area within the radius of that subswarm. This strategy is proposed in R. Brits, A. P. Engelbrecht and B. Bergh. A Niching Particle Swarm Optimizer In Proceedings of the 4th Asia-Pacific Conference on Simulated Evolution and Learning (SEAL'02), 2002, 2, 692-696
