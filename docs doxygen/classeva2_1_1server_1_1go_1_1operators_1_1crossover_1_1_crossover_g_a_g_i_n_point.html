Descripción detallada

The famous n-point crossover operator on a binary and integer genotype. Genotypes of parent individuals are recombined by exchanging subsegments within randomly selected points. Therefore, far-away allels (larger GA schemas) are more likely to be split between individuals.

Autor
mkron, streiche
