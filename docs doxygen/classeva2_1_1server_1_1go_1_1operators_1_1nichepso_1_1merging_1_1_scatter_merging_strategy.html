Descripción detallada

Two subswarms are merged if their radii overlap. In case both radii equal zero the subswarms are merged if their distance is below a given threshold mu. During merging, the particles of one of these subswarms are reinitialized into the main swarm. This improves exploration compared to the StandardMergingStrategy. This strategy is proposed in [1] and a small value, such as mu = 0.001, is suggested. [1] A.P. Engelbrecht and L.N.H. van Loggerenberg. Enhancing the nichepso. In IEEE Congress on Evolutionary Computation, 2007.
