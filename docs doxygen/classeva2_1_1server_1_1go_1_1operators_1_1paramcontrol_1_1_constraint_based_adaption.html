Descripción detallada

Simple penalty factor adaption for contraints with fitness based penalty after Bean and Hadj-Alouane 1992/1997. If the best individual was always feasible for k generations, the penalty factor is decreased, if it was always infeasible, the penalty factor is increased. For other cases, the penalty remains the same. This is plausible for the typical case that the optimum lies near the constraint boundary, however it makes the fitness function change dynamically based only on the positions of last best indidivuals.

The authors advise to select betaInc != 1./betaDec to avoid cycling.
