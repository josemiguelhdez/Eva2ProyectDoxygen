Descripción detallada

The winged MOEA was a nice idea, which didn't really work out. Here a standard MOEA is assisted by n additional local searchers, each optimizing just one objective. The idea was that these local optimizers would span the search space and would allow the MOEA to converge faster. But in the end the performance of this algorithm strongly depends on the optimization problem. Created by IntelliJ IDEA.
